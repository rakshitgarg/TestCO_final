Welcome to TESTCO.

TestCo is a utility software that makes rendering online tests possible. While we endeavour to provide the TestCo framework for one such test (PESSAT), institutions can device test formats that conform to their syllabi. Questions in the test may include a myriad of types - objective, pictorial, descriptive and the like. While evaluation of objective questions is computer based, evidently, subjective questions require human scrutiny.
