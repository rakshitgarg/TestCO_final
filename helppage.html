TESTCO HELP

IF YOU ARE A STUDENT...

When you log in as a student, you are redirected to your own profile. On your profile page, there are various options for you. You can take mock tests by clicking on the "Mock Test" link. You can view the syllabus by clicking on "View syllabus". You can choose the date to take the test by entering the date and clicking on "Check for availability" button. You can also change your personal details in your profile using the "Edit profile"option.

IF YOU ARE A CONTRIBUTOR...

When you log in as a contributor, you are redirected to your own profile. From your profile, there are a number of things you can do. If you want to view the questions you have previously contributed,click on the "View contributions" button. If you want to contribute another question, click on the "Contribute" button. When you do so, you are taken to the page where you are given options on what type of questions you are to contribute - either an MCQ, or a descriptive question, or a descriptive question with diagram.

IF YOU ARE A VALIDATOR...

When you log in as a validator, you are taken to your own profile. From your profile, you can view the questions that you have already validated. You can also validate more questions.

IF YOU ARE AN EVALUATOR...

When you log in as a evaluator, you are redirected to your own profile. From your profile, there are options for you to evaluate questions, by clicking on the "continue evaluation" button and also to view the questions that you have evaluated by clicking on the "Finished" button.
