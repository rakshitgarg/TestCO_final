TestCo is an online test facility that can be used by educational institutions for conducting entrance tests for admission of students. Our project, TestCo is a utility software that makes rendering online tests possible. While we endeavour to provide the TestCo framework for one such test (PESSAT), institutions can device test formats that conform to their syllabi. Questions in the test may include a myriad of types - objective, pictorial, descriptive and the like. While evaluation of objective questions is computer based,evidently,subjective questions require human scrutiny.
